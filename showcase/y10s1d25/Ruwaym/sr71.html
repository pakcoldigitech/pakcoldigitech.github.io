SR-71 Blackbird

Key Information:

  • The Lockheed SR-71 Blackbird remains the fastest air-breathing jet in history, reaching speeds of Mach 3.3+. It was designed for reconnaissance missions, flying so fast that no enemy missile could reach it.
  • Top Speed: Mach 3.3 (3,550+ kmph)
  • Max Altitude: 85,000 ft
  • Built with 85% titanium to withstand extreme heat.
  • Fun Fact: The SR-71 leaked fuel on the ground but sealed in the air due to expansion.
