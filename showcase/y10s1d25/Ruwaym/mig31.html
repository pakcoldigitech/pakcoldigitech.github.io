MiG-31 Foxhound

Key Information:

  • The MiG-31 Foxhound is a highly advanced Soviet interceptor, capable of carrying hypersonic missiles and engaging multiple targets at once.
  • Top Speed: Mach 2.8
  • Max Altitude: 67,500 ft
  • Equipped with long-range radar and advanced weaponry.
  • Fun Fact: The MiG-31 was the first Soviet jet with look-down/shoot-down radar capability.
