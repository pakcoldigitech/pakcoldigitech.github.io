XB-70 Valkyrie

Key Information:

  • The XB-70 Valkyrie was an experimental bomber capable of reaching Mach 3 speeds, designed to outrun any interceptor aircraft of its time.
  • Top Speed: Mach 3.1
  • Max Altitude: 77,350 ft
  • Built for nuclear strike missions but never entered service.
  • Fun Fact: The XB-70 used "compression lift," where shockwaves helped increase lift at high speeds.
