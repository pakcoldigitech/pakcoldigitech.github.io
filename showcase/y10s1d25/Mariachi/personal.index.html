Porsche 911

The Porsche 911 is an iconic sports car with over 50 years of history. Known for its powerful engine and exceptional handling, it remains one of the most respected and admired cars in the world.

  • Engine: 3.0L Turbocharged 6-cylinder
  • Power: 443 hp
  • Top Speed: 191 mph
  • Acceleration: 0-60 mph in 3.4 seconds

Porsche 918 Spyder

The Porsche 918 Spyder is a hybrid supercar that combines incredible performance with cutting-edge technology. It’s a rare and exceptional car, representing Porsche’s push into the future of hybrid technology.

  • Engine: 4.6L V8 Hybrid
  • Power: 887 hp
  • Top Speed: 214 mph
  • Acceleration: 0-60 mph in 2.2 seconds

Porsche Taycan

The Porsche Taycan is Porsche’s first fully electric sports sedan. With its futuristic design and impressive range, it marks a new era for the brand.

  • Engine: Dual Electric Motors
  • Power: 616 hp (Turbo version: 750 hp)
  • Top Speed: 161 mph
  • Acceleration: 0-60 mph in 2.6 seconds
