Motor Bikes

Why I like Motorbikes

Every second weekend me and my family go to a car show. No matter where it is, or how far away we always go without fail. As the youngest ive never really had a choice weither or weither not to go, and me being your typical girly girl I hated it. However over time I eventually started to like car shows, but not for the cars, for the bikes, rows and rows of almost every bike you can think of. Everytime we go to these car shows instead of looking at the cars and debating with the rest of the family, if a car had real carbon fibre or if it was just a wrap, I was off gawking at the bikes.

My favorite was the Yamaha R6 and the Ducati V4, not just because the first time I first saw one of them it was custom and pink, but because they look so cool, I did my research and began to like them even more. Go on to About Favorites to find out more about them
